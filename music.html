About my Music

I have self taught myself to make electronic dance music over the years. My primary focus is my degree in computing science, but in my spare time I learn and dabble with creating EDM. I use FL Studio 20 as my Digital Audio Workstation (DAW). My favourite generator plugin is the Sylenth1 synth and my favourite effects plugin is probably FL Maximus. I use Audio Technica ATH-m50X headphones and Rokit 5 studio monitors as my output devices

My favourite sub genre is progressive house, or maybe it is not, I am not really sure where my particular taste falls in terms of sub genre names, which makes it hard to google for new music I will like. Some of my absolute favourite artists are Matthew Parker, Martin Garrix, Hellberg, Built by Titan, and Capital Kings. Search any of their songs and you might get an idea for my favourite style of music.

I used to put my music online under the alias 'CMack.' However, for any future postings I plan to just use my name, I don't really like the name CMack anymore (Also there is another CMack, who makes music as well. Though a very different style).
