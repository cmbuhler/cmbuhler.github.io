About me

Hello, I am Chase, a computing science student at the University of Alberta!

I am currently in between my third and fourth year of study and am interning in the Faculty of Science. My current role is a web application developer. The experience has been a great intro to work life in the field of CS and has given me valuable insight into where I may want to take my career.

I enjoy all things technology and get really excited when learning how things work! I particularly enjoy the science and technology behind computer animation and 3D graphics. I am starting to figure out that this area of study is most interesting to me and I am excited for the learning journey ahead! I like to say I enjoy the art of computing science. I say this not to say I am an artist, because I am really bad at that, but to say I enjoy making tools that will help artists make their work easier and inspire them to create bigger and better projects!

Outside of CS related things, I enjoy making and listening to electronic dance music (or EDM for short). I am completely self taught in the music department thanks to many hours of youtube content, but I feel I am a pretty decent artist (Though I have a long way to go haha). I also enjoy going to the movies, playing games, and volunteering at my church (I am currently a camera operator, its quite fun!).

This is my personal sight, somehow you stumbled upon it. It houses a variety of things I have worked on and want to share. Feel free to use the menu to find the stuff your looking for!
