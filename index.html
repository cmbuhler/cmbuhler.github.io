About me

Hello, my name is Chase!

I am recent graduate in computing science from the University of Alberta. My resume includes web development positions at the University of Alberta, Faculty of Science and at the University of Alberta Students' Union (UASU). In the Faculty of Science, I focused on the development and advancement of internal applications to automate routine administrative tasks for the Office of The Dean. Five other students and I initially developed the prototype for the UASU Perks application (perks.uasu.ca). Afterward, I was exclusivly hired to further the development of Perks and bring it to market.

I enjoy all things technology and get really excited when learning how things work! Over the last year and a half working in web development, I have developed an expertise and enjoyment of the field that has inspired me to learn more about building web applications and utilizing cloud computing. I also enjoy the science and technology behind computer animation and 3D graphics. I am starting to figure out that this area of study is most interesting to me and I am excited for the learning journey ahead!

Outside of CS related things, I enjoy making and listening to electronic dance music (or EDM for short). I am completely self taught in the music department thanks to many hours of youtube content, but I feel I am a pretty decent artist (Though I have a long way to go haha). I also enjoy going to the movies, playing games, and volunteering at my church (I am currently a camera operator, its quite fun!).

This is my personal sight, somehow you stumbled upon it. It houses a variety of things I have worked on, such as some of my 3D art, music creations, and more. Feel free to use the menu to find the stuff your looking for!

Please note all comments and thoughts are my own! Thanks for reading!
